Main Spots in India

Greeny - Mountains

Sinhagad Fort, near Pune, becomes a lush green paradise during the rainy season. The monsoon enhances its scenic beauty with vibrant greenery, cascading waterfalls, and misty views, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers.

Sunny - Beaches

Sky above, sand below, peace within. Look up at the cerulean blue sky. Feel the warm sugar sand between your toes. Find inner peace with all of the Gulf Coast beauty that surrounds you. It's so easy to be relaxed here.
